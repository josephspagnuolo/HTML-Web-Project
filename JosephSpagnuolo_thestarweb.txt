<tr valign="top"><td style="padding-top:0; line-height:1.6;"><p><font face="georgia" size="5" color="333333">After the tremendous sales of the PS2 and PS3
 home gaming consoles, <a href="http://www.sony.ca/en" target="new">Sony</a> was determined to top the success of their prior products with its next console. 
Development of this eighth generation console began in 2008, according to <a href="https://twitter.com/cerny?lang=en" target="new">Mark Cerny</a>, the PS4's 
system architect.</font></p></td></tr>
<tr valign="top"><td style="padding-top:0; line-height:1.6;"><p><font face="georgia" size="5" color="333333">
Leading up to release, Sony worked with other companies to improve the PS4.
At a Gamescom event in Germany, Sony announced the launch date of November 15, 2013 for the PS4. Because of its improvements from the PS3, the PS4 had great
 sales, and reached 40 million consoles sold in 2016.</font></p>

</td></tr>

<tr><td style="padding-top:0; line-height:1.6;">
<img src="images/vrheadset.jpg" alt="PlayStation VR" width="280" height="276" align="right">
<p><font face="georgia" size="5" color="333333">The Dualshock 4 controller was improved from its predecessor through feedback on how to design it more
 efficiently. Sony recently launched an accessory called the PS VR on October 13, 2016 to make the PS4 experience more inclusive.
The <a href="https://www.playstation.com/en-ca/explore/playstation-vr/" target="new">PlayStation VR</a> is a virtual reality headset from Sony that is 
compatible with the PS4. With a 100 degree field of view and 1080p resolution, it revolutionizes how the PS4 is used.</font></p>
</td></tr>
<tr><td style="line-height:1.6; padding-top:0;">
<p><font face="georgia" size="5" color="333333">Sony additionally created Remote Play. This allows the consumer to connect their Dualshock 4 controller 
to their <a href="http://www.sonymobile.com/ca-en/" target="new">Sony Xperia</a> and play PS4 remotely on their phone.</font></p>
</td></tr>
